Help
Widgets

Here you can select the available widgets, click on them to activate or deactivate them. Active widgets will turn green, inactive ones will be white.

Account and Personalization

Personalize your Ultidash in this section: choose your favorite font, select your preferences and manage your account.

Background

In this section you can upload your favorite pictures and use them as background for your new tab. Upload as many as you want and decide how often you want them to rotate!

Greetings

The Greetings are those motivational, funny or just random messages shown below the time. They are all generated by Ultidash’ users to keep you motivated and make you smile! In this section you can contribute with your creativity and anonymously submit Greetings to the Ultidash community. You can also write your own private list of Greetings to remind yourself of what matters to you. Tip: activate the “Safe for Work Filter” to block unsafe greetings.

Q&A

How often do pictures and Greetings change?

The background picture changes twice a day at 6 am (day picture) and at 6 pm (night picture), while the Greetings change every hour Tip: you can skip greetings in by clicking on the round arrow that appears when you hover over them.

Can I resize the Todo widget?

Sure, just move the cursor to its top-left corner and a resizing arrow will appear.

How does the Matrix Todo work?

“What is important is seldom urgent and what is urgent is seldom important” - Dwight Eisenhower
The Matrix Todo is based on the Eisenhower Box, a useful framework to separate and prioritize your tasks. Always try to do first what’s in box 1, and then 2, 3 and 4, in this order. The goal is to filter out the noise and focus on what really matters.

How should I use the Concentration Timer?

“A goal is a dream with a deadline” - Napoleon Hill
The Concentration Timer is a powerful tool to allocate a fixed time period to each planned activity. This ensures that you finish your task within the allocated time or that you don't spend too long on a task that isn't worth the effort. Tip: if you click on “Show Stats” button, you will be able to see how long you have used the Concentration Timer for the current day and overall. You can also set daily concentration goals and monitor your performance over time.

What is the Site Blocker?

The Site Blocker allows you to temporarily block time-wasting websites. If you activate the Site Blocker, you will be redirected to the new tab page whenever you try to reach a blacklisted website. If you select “Activate with Concentration Timer”, the Site Blocker will be activated automatically whenever the timer is running.

What is the Site Tracker?

The Site Tracker gives you useful insights on how you spend your time on the web in a simple pie-chart visualization. It’s useful to look at it at the end of the day to see whether your time online is used productively.

I submitted a Greeting but I don’t see it in the dashboard, why?

All submitted Greetings are reviewed and are added to the public list at the discretion of the Ultidash team.

What about privacy?

The only data we collect is what is asked during registration (i.e. name and email). All the rest is stored locally on your Chrome local storage, and we have no access to it. Read more about it here: http://goo.gl/qUkxio

How do I remove the extension from Chrome?

On your browser, click menu. Select More tools > Extensions. Next to the extension Ultidash, click Remove from Chrome. A notice to remove the extension will appear. Click Remove. Alternatively, if you see the Ultidash icon in your Chrome toolbar, you can right-click on the icon and select Remove from Chrome.
